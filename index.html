About

Who we are

Nystara is a reading app built for people with nystagmus — a condition that causes involuntary eye movement, making it physically exhausting to read across a page of text. The app delivers text one word at a time to a single fixed point on your screen, so your eyes never have to move.

Nystara is an independent reading app based in Los Angeles, California.

The short version

What this policy covers

Nystara does not collect, transmit, sell, or share your personal data. Everything the app stores — your reading preferences, your place in a document, and the text of documents you upload — stays on your device, encrypted. Nothing is sent to us or to any third party. Ever.

This privacy policy explains in detail what data the app stores on your device, how it is protected, how long it is kept, and how you can delete it. We are required to publish this policy by Apple's App Store and Google Play, even though we collect no external data.

Security

How your data is protected

All data stored by Nystara is encrypted using your device's own security systems. We do not have access to your data — not because we choose not to look, but because it is technically impossible for us to access it.

  • Reading preferences and session data are stored using MMKV, an encrypted key-value storage library that uses AES-128 encryption.
  • Document content is stored using your device's hardware-backed encryption: iOS Keychain on Apple devices, Android Keystore on Android devices. This is the same system used to protect your passwords and payment information.
  • No data is ever transmitted over a network. There is no server. There is no database. There is nothing to breach on our end.

What we do not do

Data we never collect

To be completely explicit about what Nystara does not do:

  • We do not collect your name, email address, or any account information. There is no account. There is no login.
  • We do not collect your location.
  • We do not use analytics tools (no Google Analytics, no Firebase, no Mixpanel, no Amplitude).
  • We do not use crash reporting services that send data off your device.
  • We do not use advertising networks or tracking pixels.
  • We do not share any data with third parties — because there is no data to share.
  • We do not collect information about your medical condition, vision status, or diagnosis.
  • We do not run background processes that collect data when you are not using the app.

Age

Children's privacy

Nystara does not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 13. The app does not require an account or any personal information from any user of any age. Parents and educators may use Nystara on behalf of children with nystagmus — no data about the child is transmitted or stored beyond the encrypted local preferences described above.

Your rights

Your rights and choices

Because all data is stored locally on your device, you have complete control at all times.

  1. 1 View what's stored: Go to Settings → Privacy → View my data. You will see exactly what Nystara has stored on your device.
  2. 2 Delete everything: Go to Settings → Privacy → Delete all my data. This permanently removes all preferences, session history, and document content from your device. This action cannot be undone.
  3. 3 Delete the app: Uninstalling Nystara from your device permanently removes all data the app has stored. Nothing remains on our servers because nothing was ever sent there.
  4. 4 Delete a specific document: In your document list, swipe left on any document and tap Delete. The document content is permanently removed from encrypted storage.

California

California residents (CCPA)

If you are a California resident, the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA) gives you rights over your personal information. Because Nystara does not collect, sell, or share personal information, most CCPA provisions do not apply in a traditional sense. However, to be explicit:

  • We do not sell your personal information to any third party.
  • We do not share your personal information for cross-context behavioral advertising.
  • You have the right to know what personal information is collected about you. Our answer: none, beyond what is stored locally on your device as described in this policy.
  • You have the right to delete your personal information. Instructions are in the "Your rights and choices" section above.

To exercise any CCPA rights or ask questions, contact us at the email address below.

European users

GDPR — European Economic Area

If you are located in the European Economic Area (EEA), the United Kingdom, or Switzerland, the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) may apply to you. Because Nystara processes no personal data on our servers, we are not a data controller or data processor in the traditional GDPR sense. All processing occurs locally on your device under your sole control.

If you have questions about your rights under GDPR, contact us at the email below and we will respond within 30 days.
